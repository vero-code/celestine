Our Mission

Celestine was born from a profound question: How can we make the vast complexity of space exploration accessible to everyone?

By leveraging Multi-Agent Systems and Gemini 2.5, we translate geological data from distant worlds into familiar Earth contexts.

We measure impact through SDG 4 (Quality Education) and SDG 9 (Innovation), making advanced planetary science accessible via consumer technology.
